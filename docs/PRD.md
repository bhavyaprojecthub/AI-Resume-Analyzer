# AI Resume Analyzer
## Problem Statement

Job seekers often apply to jobs without knowing:

- Resume quality
- ATS compatibility
- Missing skills
- Resume-job match percentage

This leads to low interview conversion rates.


## Solution

Build an AI Resume Analyzer that:

- Parses resumes
- Parses job descriptions
- Calculates ATS score
- Detects missing skills
- Generates interview questions
- Suggests resume improvements

---

## Target Users

### Students

Need internship preparation.

### Job Seekers

Need better resume optimization.

### Recruiters

Need faster candidate evaluation.

---

## Success Criteria

- Accurate resume parsing
- ATS analysis
- Skill gap detection
- Interview preparation
